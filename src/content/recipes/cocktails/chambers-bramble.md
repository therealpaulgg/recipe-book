---
excerpt: Gin and black raspberry Chambord liqueur
title: Chamber's Bramble
category: cocktails
tags: [tart]
rating: 8

ingredients:
- 1.5oz gin (Aviation Gin)
- 1oz lemon juice
- 0.5oz Chambord
- 0.5oz Simple Syrup

directions:
- Pour into a shaker with ice and shake for 30 seconds
- Strain into a martini glass and add raspberries.
